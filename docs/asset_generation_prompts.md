# 에셋 생성 프롬프트 마스터 리스트 (Phase 4)

## 공통 표준 (Common Standards)
- **Background**: `Pure black atmospheric background, void-like depth, no floor, no horizon.`
- **Quality**: `Dark fantasy art style, cinematic lighting, high quality digital painting, mastery illustration, sharp focus, 8k resolution, extreme details, masterpiece.`
- **Negative Prompt**: `circular borders, white background, frames, text, labels, watermark, cartoonish, low quality, messy background, landscape scenery.`
